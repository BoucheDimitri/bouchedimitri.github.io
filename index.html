About me

Since October 2019, I am a PhD candidate at Télécom Paris Télécom Paris under the supervision of Florence d'Alché-Buc, Marianne Clausel, and François Roueff working on efficient methods to predict functions with applications to spatio-temporal data.

Before that, I graduated from ENSAE and M2 MVA from ENS Paris-Saclay.

Research Interests

I'm globally interested in Machine Learning, and for the moment focus my attention on

  • Operator-Valued Kernels and integral Operators

  • Efficient learning for function-valued outputs

  • Applications of Machine Learning to impactful problems (renewable energy, medicine, …)
